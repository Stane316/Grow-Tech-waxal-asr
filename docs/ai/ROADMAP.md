# 1. Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term vision, strategic milestones and progressive evolution of the Google WAXAL ASR Challenge project.

Unlike the project backlog (`TASKS.md`), which focuses on short-term execution, this roadmap describes **where the project is going** and **how each milestone contributes to the long-term vision of Grow Tech AI**.

Every AI agent and team member should use this document to understand the project's strategic direction before planning new work.

---

# 2. Vision

The Google WAXAL ASR Challenge is not viewed as a standalone competition.

It is the first large-scale research project of the **Grow Tech AI Research Lab**, designed to establish a reusable ecosystem for Speech AI, multilingual AI and future hackathons.

The long-term objective is to transform this repository into a modular research platform that can accelerate future AI projects.

---

# 3. Strategic Objectives

The roadmap pursues five complementary objectives:

### Research Excellence

Develop a deep understanding of modern Automatic Speech Recognition systems and multilingual speech technologies.

---

### Engineering Excellence

Build a modular, extensible and maintainable AI research framework.

---

### Competition Performance

Design models capable of achieving strong performance and generalization on the Google WAXAL ASR Challenge.

---

### Knowledge Capitalization

Transform every experiment, decision and reusable component into long-term knowledge assets.

---

### Ecosystem Growth

Lay the foundations for future Grow Tech AI projects, hackathons and open-source initiatives.

# 8. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk                  | Impact | Mitigation                              |
| --------------------- | ------ | --------------------------------------- |
| Overfitting           | High   | Cross-validation and hidden-set mindset |
| Poor documentation    | Medium | Documentation-first workflow            |
| Technical debt        | High   | Modular architecture and reviews        |
| Untracked experiments | High   | Experiment Log and Decision Log         |
| Scope creep           | Medium | Prioritize roadmap phases               |

# 9. Guiding Principles

The roadmap follows these principles:

- Build reusable assets.
- Prefer reproducibility over shortcuts.
- Optimize only after validation.
- Document every important decision.
- Think beyond the current competition.
