# 1. Purpose of this Document

This document serves as the official knowledge base of the Grow Tech AI Research Lab.

It centralizes every external and internal reference used throughout the project.

Its objectives are to:

- preserve technical knowledge;
- facilitate onboarding;
- justify engineering decisions;
- ensure reproducibility;
- prevent information loss.

Every important external resource should be referenced here.
